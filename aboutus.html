About Us


The Orange Gallery in Belize was founded by the Sherrard family, who are passionate about promoting local art and culture. Nestled in the heart of Belize, our gallery serves as a vibrant hub for artists and art enthusiasts alike. With a focus on showcasing the rich diversity of Belizean artistry, we curate exhibitions that celebrate the country's vibrant culture, stunning landscapes, and unique heritage. Through our gallery, we aim to foster a deep appreciation for art while supporting local artists in their creative endeavors. Join us on a journey of discovery and inspiration as we invite you to explore the colorful world of Belizean art at the Orange Gallery.
